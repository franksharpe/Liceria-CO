About us

Liceria & Co, an Italian restaurant established in 1990, has become a beloved culinary institution known for its commitment to authentic Italian flavors and warm hospitality. Over the decades, it has consistently delighted diners with its delectable dishes, creating memorable experiences for patrons of all ages

Culinary Excellence: Liceria & Co has earned a reputation for its culinary excellence. The menu features a wide array of traditional Italian dishes prepared with the finest ingredients. From handcrafted pasta to wood-fired pizzas, every dish is a testament to the restaurant's dedication to preserving the essence of Italian cuisine.

Warm and Inviting Atmosphere: The restaurant's inviting ambiance is reminiscent of a charming trattoria in the heart of Italy. Cozy dim lighting, rustic décor, and the soothing notes of Italian music combine to create an unforgettable dining experience. Whether it's a romantic date night or a family celebration, Liceria & Co provides the perfect setting.

Family Tradition: Liceria & Co is a family-owned restaurant, and this personal touch is evident in every aspect of the dining experience. The warmth and friendliness of the staff make guests feel like they are part of the Liceria family, creating a sense of belonging and comfort.

Signature Dishes: Over the years, certain dishes at Liceria & Co have achieved legendary status among regulars. The homemade lasagna, featuring layers of rich, velvety béchamel and flavorful Bolognese sauce, is a perennial favorite. Meanwhile, the tiramisu, made from a closely guarded family recipe, is the perfect sweet ending to any meal.

Evolution and Innovation: While rooted in tradition, Liceria & Co has also evolved to meet the changing tastes of its patrons. The restaurant regularly introduces seasonal menus that incorporate fresh, locally sourced ingredients, ensuring that each visit is a unique experience.

Awards

  1. 1995 Michelin Star: Awarded for excellence in cuisine, service, and ambiance.
  2. 1997 James Beard Award: Recognized for outstanding Italian culinary achievements in the United States.
  3. 2002 Gourmet Italia Award: Presented to restaurants that excel in delivering authentic and innovative Italian flavors.
  4. 2004 Wine Spectator Award of Excellence: Acknowledged for an impressive wine selection that complements the Italian cuisine.
  5. 2007 Italian Heritage Award: Honored for preserving and promoting Italian culinary traditions.
  6. 2010 Golden Olive Branch: A prestigious award for maintaining consistently high-quality olive oil in their dishes.
  7. 2013 Culinary Innovator Award: Recognized for introducing unique and creative Italian dishes to the culinary world.
  8. 2015 Dolce Vita Dessert Award: Celebrated for their delectable Italian desserts and pastries.
  9. 2018 Pasta Perfection Award: Praised for their mastery in crafting and serving perfect pasta dishes.
  10. 2020 Timeless Elegance Award: Acknowledged for maintaining a classic and sophisticated ambiance that transports diners to Italy.
